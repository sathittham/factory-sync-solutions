## About FactorySync Solutions (English)

FactorySync Solutions is a factory readiness assessment platform (Factory Health Check) that
helps factory owners and managers understand their strengths, improvement areas, and
development roadmap through an online assessment designed by specialist engineers.

### Core Services

1. **Factory Health Check** — an 8-dimension readiness assessment (the "Shindan" methodology)
   with a report and improvement plan.
2. **Production System Assessment** — inspects production systems, machinery, and processes to
   improve efficiency.
3. **Efficiency Improvement Consulting** — analyzes problems, defines approaches, and drives
   measurable improvements.
4. **Digital Factory & Smart Dashboard** — an online KPI tracking system with data integration
   and AI-powered analytics.

### The 8-Dimension Assessment (Shindan)

Basic Management · Work Improvement · Coordination · Maintenance · Quality · Production ·
Material · Cost.

Factories receive a score per dimension, a radar chart comparing them against an industry
benchmark, and in-depth recommendations.

### Other Available Quiz Variants

- **Factory Assessment** — a general factory assessment
- **Cybersecurity Assessment** — evaluates a factory's cybersecurity posture
- **Lean Business Assessment** — evaluates lean business practices
- **ISO 29110 Software Process Assessment** — assesses software development processes against
  the ISO 29110 standard (for small software teams)

### How to Register and Take an Assessment

1. Sign up on the web app (app.factorysyncsolutions.com) with email or Google Sign-In.
2. Fill in company details (company name, 13-digit registration ID, industry type, company
   size, contact information).
3. Choose the assessment to take (e.g. Shindan, Factory, Cybersecurity, Lean, ISO 29110).
4. Answer every dimension's questions — the score is calculated automatically on submission.

### Where Results Appear

Results (per-dimension scores, a radar chart, strengths, weaknesses, and an overall diagnosis)
appear on the account's "Results" page immediately after submission, and past assessments remain
viewable in history. If email notifications are enabled, a summary is also emailed to the
registered address.
